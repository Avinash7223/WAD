Artificial Intelligrnce

the ability of a digital computer or computer-controlled robot to perform tasks commonly associated with intelligent beings.

What is intelligence?

All but the simplest human behaviour is ascribed to intelligence, while even the most complicated insect behaviour is usually not taken as an indication of intelligence.

Artificial general intelligence (AGI), applied AI, and cognitive simulation
